About Bihar

Bihar is a state located in eastern India, known for its historical significance and rich cultural heritage. From the ancient university of Nalanda to the religious importance of Bodh Gaya, Bihar has always been a land of knowledge and spirituality. The state's diverse culture, local traditions, and delicious cuisine make it a must-visit for travelers seeking an authentic Indian experience.
